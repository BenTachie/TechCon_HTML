Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 as a small local meetup for developers and engineers. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most anticipated annual tech events worldwide, attracting thousands of professionals.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we believe in empowering innovation, fostering collaboration, and bridging the gap between emerging technologies and the people shaping them. Our mission is to inspire, educate, and connect tech enthusiasts globally.
